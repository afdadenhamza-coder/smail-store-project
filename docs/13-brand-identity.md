# 13 — Brand Identity & Positioning

## Brand Name
**Smail Store** — أصيل in spirit, modern in execution. Approachable name that carries warmth and confidence.

## Tagline
**جودة عالمية، روح مغربية** — International Quality, Moroccan Soul

## Identity
The first premium Moroccan streetwear brand built on trust, not hype. No dropshipping, no scams, no fake discounts — just certified quality at honest prices, with packaging and service that rival international brands.

We're the Moroccan alternative to Carhartt WIP, Nike, and Essentials Fear of God — at prices that make sense for Moroccan men.

## Brand Positioning

| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Promise** | Premium quality you can trust — certified, tested, guaranteed |
| **Voice** | Confident, warm, direct (Darija, Arabic script) |
| **Vibe** | Premium streetwear × Moroccan pride. Think dark, earthy, confident |
| **Differentiator** | First Moroccan streetwear brand with quality certification + premium packaging |
| **Target** | 18-40 urban Moroccan men, style-conscious, tired of IG scams and cheap imports |

## Key Messaging Pillars

1. **الجودة المعتمدة (Certified Quality)** — "ما كاينش أحسن من هاد الجودة فالمغرب"
2. **الثقة (Trust)** — "أكثر من 500 راجل واثق فينا، وخدمتنا معروفة"
3. **الذوق المغربي (Moroccan Style)** — "لبس يعكس شخصيتك وهويتك"
4. **السرعة (Speed)** — "التوصيل ف 3 حتي 5 أيام فجميع مدن المغرب"

## Color Palette

| Token | Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-------|-----|-------|
| brand-black | Deep Black | `#0A0A0A` | Primary backgrounds, text |
| brand-black-light | Charcoal | `#1A1A1A` | Card backgrounds, hover states |
| brand-terracotta | Warm Clay | `#C07040` | Primary accent, CTAs, highlights |
| brand-terracotta-light | Light Clay | `#D4885A` | Hover states, secondary accents |
| brand-sand | Warm Sand | `#F5E6D3` | Warm backgrounds, trust sections |
| surface | Light Gray | `#F5F5F5` | Neutral backgrounds |
| text-secondary | Medium Gray | `#666666` | Body text |
| text-muted | Light Gray | `#999999` | Secondary/muted text |

- Terracotta was chosen over gold for: deeper Moroccan identity (clay architecture, earth), stronger authority (less flashy, more grounded), and distinctiveness in the streetwear space.

## Tone & Language

- **Confident but warm** — like a friend who knows style
- **Direct, no fluff** — "هاد هو اللي بغيتي" not long descriptions
- **Pride in quality** — "جرب، و إلا رد ليك فلوسك" (Try it, or your money back)
- **Scarcity with honesty** — "الإنتاج محدود باش نضمنو الجودة" (Limited production to guarantee quality)

## Competitive Positioning

| vs | Smail Store | Other IG stores | International brands |
|---|---|---|---|
| Quality | Certified premium | Unknown/unreliable | High but expensive |
| Price | Honest, fair | Cheap = cheap quality | 3-5× more expensive |
| Trust | Real brand, real address | PO box / no address | Global but no local feel |
| Culture | Moroccan pride | Generic | Not Moroccan |
| Packaging | Premium unboxing | Plastic bag | Standard |

## Visual Direction

- Dark and earthy — black foundations, terracotta accents, sand warmth
- Minimalist — products are the hero, not the design
- Moroccan texture — use clay tones, natural materials in imagery
- Gold-tier unboxing — rigid box, tissue paper, care card, scent strip
