# 02 — Brand Positioning & ICP

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Gender | Male |
| Age | 18–40 |
| Location | Morocco (Casablanca, Rabat, Marrakech, Tangier, Fes) |
| Income | Middle class and above |
| Style | Modern, streetwear, clean fits, trendy |
| Pain | Has been scammed by IG dropshippers before; wants quality but no local trusted option |
| Behavior | Uses Instagram/TikTok daily, shops online, uses COD |
| Language | Darija (Moroccan Arabic) — written in Arabic script |
| Device | Mobile-first (>80%) |

## Brand Voice

- **Confident** — "أنا الراجل" energy (I am the man)
- **Direct** — No fluff, no fake discounts
- **Exclusive** — "هاد الفوت ما غادي يجي بزاف" (this opportunity won't come often)
- **Trust-building** — "واثقين من الجودة ديالنا" (we are confident in our quality)
- **Social proof constantly** — "كثر من 500 راجل واخد الطلب ديالهم" (500+ men placed their order)

## Key Messaging Pillars

1. **Jawda (Quality/الجودة)** — "ما كاينش أحسن من هاد الجودة عند أي حد"
2. **Thiqa (Trust/الثقة)** — "خدمنا معروف و سمعتنا فالمغرب"
3. **Zbi (Style/الذوق)** — "لبس يعكس شخصيتك"
4. **Sari3 (Speed/السرعة)** — "التوصيل ف 3 حتي 5 أيام"

## Positioning Statement

> "Smail Store is the first Moroccan streetwear brand that delivers premium quality with real trust. No scams. No fake discounts. Just the best quality clothes at honest prices, delivered to your door."
